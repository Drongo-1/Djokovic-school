    • DJOKOVIC SCHOOL OF PROGRAMMING

    Djokovic school of programmers is a public research university in Africa. Founded in 1881 with the support of local industrialists and inventors especially Edward Weston,DCP opened as Newark Technical School in 1885 with 88 students.The school grew into a classic engineering college – Newark College of Engineering – and then, with the addition of a School of Architecture in 1973, into a polytechnic university that now hosts five colleges and one school.[13] As of fall 2019, the university enrolls about 11,500 students, 2,000 of whom live on campus. DCP offers (Bachelor of Computer science)(Masters and PhD) programs.
